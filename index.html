7 free AI tools that do 90% of the work
Copy.ai (copywriting), Canva AI (design), ElevenLabs (voice), Descript (editing), Perplexity (research), ChatGPT (planning), Runway (video).

The exact content pipeline: Idea → Published
Research → Script → Record → Edit → Repurpose → Schedule. Each step has a free AI tool that cuts the time in half.

When to upgrade (and when NOT to)
Free tiers handle 90% of what you need. Upgrade only when: (1) you're posting 5+ times/week, (2) you need custom branding, (3) collaboration features save you more than the subscription costs.

8 secrets to grow faster
Hook in 3s, pattern interrupt every 15s, one takeaway per piece, remix top content, post consistently, engage before posting, test different formats, track what works and double down.

30-day battle plan
Week 1: Set up tools & templates. Week 2: Produce 10 pieces of content. Week 3: Test & optimize. Week 4: Scale what works.
